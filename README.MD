## 🚀 About Me / What I do

I am an **AI Engineer & Data Scientist** with over 6 years of industry experience across machine learning development and robust data architecture. Having completed my MS in Data Science (3.95 GPA) at Indiana University in Jan 2026, my work bridges the gap between deep data engineering platforms and intelligent algorithmic logic. With extensive experience as an AI Engineer at The Polis Center, an AI Data Engineer Intern at Horizon League, and a Senior Data Automation Engineer at Eurofins, I specialize in building mission-critical ML pipelines, semantic LLM applications, and robust end-to-end data systems.

### 💡 Core Expertise
- **AI Agents & Knowledge Graphs:** Developing sophisticated retrieval pipelines (RAG) and graph networks (Neo4j + LangGraph), backed by Hugging Face (AI Agents) and Neo4j (Graph Data Science) certifications. Fine-tuning foundational models (GPT-4o, BERT). 
- **Applied ML & MLOps:** Architecting and deploying high-performance predictive models leveraging PyTorch, Scikit-learn, ViT, LightGBM, and time-series architectures. Scaling models to production via FastAPI, Docker, and comprehensive MLflow tracking.
- **Data Engineering & Cloud Automation:** Designing scalable ETL/ELT workflows, orchestrating cloud operations (Azure Data Factory, GCP BigQuery, Airflow), and engineering real-time streaming infrastructure (Kafka, PySpark). Certified IBM Data Engineering Professional.
